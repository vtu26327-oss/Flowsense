Blood Type Guide

Understand what your menstrual blood reveals about your health.

Bright Red

Healthy/Normal flow. Signaling fresh blood flow from the uterus.

Dark Red

Normal blood that has reacted with oxygen inside the uterus longer.

Brown

Typical old blood. Common at the very beginning or end of your period.

Blackish

Alert! Could signal infection or a long-term blockage. Consult a doctor.
